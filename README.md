# Stackers

Track gold prices and manage your precious metals portfolio on your phone.

## Features

- **Live Gold Prices** — View real-time gold prices in your preferred currency (GBP, USD, EUR, etc.) and unit (oz, kg, g)
- **Portfolio Tracking** — Keep track of your gold holdings with purchase details, weights, and premiums
- **Price History** — View historical gold price data with interactive charts
- **Local Storage** — All your data stays on your device

## Getting Started

### First Launch

On first launch, the app will fetch the latest gold price. To enable live price updates:

1. Tap the **Settings** tab (gear icon)
2. Enter your API key
3. Tap **Save**

Get a free API key at [metals.dev](https://metals.dev)

### Currency & Units

In Settings, you can choose:
- **Currency**: GBP, USD, EUR, AUD, CAD, CHF, CNY, JPY, INR
- **Unit**: troy ounces (toz), kilograms (kg), grams (g)

### Adding Items

1. Tap the **Add** tab (plus icon)
2. Enter the item details:
   - Code (e.g., "1oz Gold Bar")
   - Weight
   - Purchase Price
   - Premium over spot
3. Optionally add a photo
4. Tap **Add to Stack**

### Viewing Your Stack

The **Stack** tab shows all your gold items with:
- Total portfolio value
- Total weight
- Individual item details

### Price Charts

The **Charts** tab displays:
- Historical gold price data
- Price trends over time
- Daily changes

## Data

All data is stored locally on your device in a SQLite database. Uninstalling the app will delete all stored data.
