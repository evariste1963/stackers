# Stackers — Setup Guide

## How to set up Stackers after installing

1. **Open the app**. On first launch, you will see the Guide screen explaining app features. Tap **Set up Options** to go to Settings.

2. **Set your preferences** in Settings (optional — defaults are fine to start):
   - **Theme**: Dark or Light
   - **Currency**: GBP (£), USD ($), or EUR (€)
   - **Weight Unit**: Troy Ounce (ozt) or Gram (g)
   - **Default Metal**: Gold or Silver (determines which chart shows first on Home)

3. **Choose your data source** — you need either an API key (live prices) or manual prices (off-grid):

   ### Option A: API Key (Live Prices)
   3a. Get a free API key at [goldapi.io](https://www.goldapi.io) (email registration, free tier).
   3b. In Settings, paste the key into the **API Key** field and tap **Save API Key**.

   ### Option B: Off-Grid Mode (Manual Prices)
   3c. In Settings, scroll to **Manual Prices**.
   3d. Enter a price for **Gold**, a **Gold Premium %**, a price for **Silver**, and a **Silver Premium %**. Tap **Submit Prices** (single button for both metals).
   3e. Toggle **Off Grid Mode** ON.

4. Tap **Continue** at the bottom of Settings. The Guide screen appears — tap **Continue to App** to reach the Home screen with your price data.

5. **Explore the tabs** at the bottom of the screen:
   - **Home** — Price banner, 12-month chart, your stack grid, total value
   - **Portfolio** — Aggregated holdings, profit/loss, weight totals per metal
   - **Your Stack** — All stack items, tap to edit
   - **Add-2-Stack** — Add a new item (name, weight, cost, metal, photo)
   - **Account** — Guide, Settings, PIN management, overwrite-price buttons

6. **Add your first stack item**:
   - Tap the **Add-2-Stack** tab
   - Enter a name (e.g. "2024 Britannia")
   - Enter weight and cost per unit (or total amount)
   - Select metal (Gold / Silver)
   - Take or choose a photo (optional)
   - Tap **Add to Stack**

7. **Set a PIN** (optional):
   - Go to **Account** tab → **Set PIN**
   - Enter a 4-digit PIN and confirm
   - On next launch, the app will require this PIN to unlock

8. **You're done**. Prices update automatically (API mode) or stay at your manual entries (off-grid mode). All data stays on your device.
